Russian Practice Apps

These apps are designed to help learners of Russian practice various aspects of the language. Each app presents a challenge and a button labeled "Reveal Choices." To get the most out of your practice, think of your own answer before revealing the choices. If you're unsure, use the multiple-choice options to learn.

Available Apps

Russian Verbs of Motion

Practice all tenses and aspects of verbs of motion, including prefixes for direction and intent.

Russian Nouns and Pronouns

Drill declension patterns for pronouns and common nouns across all cases.

Russian Verb Conjugation

Master regular and irregular verb forms in present, past, and future tenses.

Russian Prepositions

Learn how prepositions interact with cases in meaningful sentence contexts.
