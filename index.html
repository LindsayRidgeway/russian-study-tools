Russian Study Tools

This is a collection of browser-based tools to help you practice Russian. Each app presents a challenge sentence and offers a "Reveal Choices" button. For effective practice, try to think of the correct answer first before revealing the options. If you're not sure, feel free to learn from the choices shown. But to build fluency, your goal is to actively recall the answer before confirming it.

  • Verbs of Motion
    Practice Russian motion verbs with prefixes, aspect, tense, and translation.
  • Pronouns and Nouns
    Practice noun cases with pronouns and their agreement in gender and number.
  • Verb Conjugations
    Drill present-tense conjugations of common Russian verbs.
  • Prepositions
    Learn which cases to use with each Russian preposition and how they affect meaning.
